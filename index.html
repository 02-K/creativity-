What is Creativity ?

Through this course I was able to discover what creativity is but also learn how to use it, Creativity is essential in the business world, whether it is to find solutions, to innovate or to create a company. Creativity is what makes us different as individuals because we all have a different vision.

How do I define creativity after 6 sessions?

Thanks to the different sessions I was able to make my own definition of what creativity is, which to me is characterised by the ability to perceive the world in new ways, to make connections between apparently unrelated phenomena and to generate solutions. As for example with session 4 where we went to take pictures of La Defense we could discover the defense in another way while following a red wire.

Anyone can be creative

One important thing about this course that stuck with me is that anyone can be creative, personally I've always found myself terrible at creative and artistic activities but through this course I've been able to discover other forms of creativity and surprise myself.
