Session 5

Ad Pepsi vs Coca-Cola

Pepsi Website

With my team mate we worked on finding a creative advert and we came across this Pepsi advert which we thought was very creative, as it was interesting on several points. Firstly it was funny because it made us think it was an advert for Coca Cola because the child bought one Coca Cola but then we see that he bought 2 Coca Colas so that he could have 1 Pepsi. However it was also very insulting to the brand image of Coca Cola. It is not a direct or vulgar attack, it is a subtle ad and the child shows the innocence of the gesture.
